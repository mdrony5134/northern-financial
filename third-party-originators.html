Third-Party Originators

Partner With an Industry Leader

For 50 years, Northern Financial Brokers Leasing has been an important funding source for Third-Party Originators by helping them provide equipment financing to small businesses nationwide. We underwrite, fund, and manage our own portfolio of contracts.

Two Underwriting Platforms

Small Ticket

$5,000 – $200,000

  • — A – B – C Credits
  • — Application Only
  • — Risk Based Pricing
  • — Fast Turnaround Time
  • — Startups Accepted to $45K

Commercial

$150,000 – $1,500,000

  • — Full-financial transactions for companies with solid credit
  • — A Credits
  • — Competitive Pricing

Our Application Process

01

Submit Documents

Complete the Third-Party Originator Application and provide a resume or professional biography for each company principal.

02

Send Your Application

Email your completed application and resume(s) to tpoapplication@finpac.com. You will be notified of next steps.

03

Review Process

We review your TPO Application, credit reports, funding source references, company reviews, and industry experience.
